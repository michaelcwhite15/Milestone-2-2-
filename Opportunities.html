Student Assistant Positions at the CARIT

The CARIT is hiring Student Assistants, There are four openings for the Summer 2022 openings. In order to be considered for such a position, student has to complete some necessary
courses with an average grade requirement of a GPA more than 3.2 for undergraduate students and a GPA greater than 3.7 for graduate students in order to be considered for these possibilities. Under graduate students must have taken courses in CSE 3203 Mobile System Overview and IT 4213 Mobile Web Development. Graduate students may choose between IT 7113 Data Visualization or IT 6713 Business Intelligence.
